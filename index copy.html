WELCOME TO THE MIND LAB

This site is designed to show you a mirror of your mind. You will witness, in real time, the processes through which you understand, experience, and interact with the world. This method of active self inquiry can evolve into a radically creative exercise in self determination - an unabashed 'F*ck You' to life on autopilot. Obstacles transform into doorways, and adversaries into teachers. All it requires is practice, curiosity, and the willingness to tell yourself the truth.

Whether you experience reality as shocking, inspid, hilarious, magnificent, or mundane is entirely up to you. PRACTICE witnessing the way your mind works. Pay attention to the quality of your noticing. Ask yourself: Why do you focus on one thing as opposed to another? Be curious about the words you choose to describe what you see.
